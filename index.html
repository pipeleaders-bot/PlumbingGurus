Do you do plumbing work like installing toilets or faucets?
No — that work requires a Texas plumbing license. We handle the tile, surround, and carpentry, and coordinate with a licensed plumber for any water or gas line connections.

Do you install customer-supplied vanities or cabinets?
Yes, for the carpentry and fitting portion.

Plumbing Gurus LLC provides tile, flooring, drywall, and carpentry services. We are not a licensed plumbing company. Any work requiring a Texas plumbing license — including fixture, water line, or gas line connections — is performed by a licensed plumbing partner, not by Plumbing Gurus LLC directly.
