Connecting You to Licensed Plumbing Professionals

Plumbing Gurus is a San Antonio–based plumbing coordination and dispatch service. We connect customers with independently licensed plumbing professionals for regulated plumbing work.

In limited situations, we may assist with minor, non-invasive maintenance tasks that do not modify plumbing systems.

How Plumbing Gurus Works

Request Service

Submit a service request online or by phone. We gather basic information to determine whether licensed plumbing is required.

We Coordinate

When plumbing work is required, we coordinate and dispatch an independently licensed plumber in your area.

Work Performed

All regulated plumbing work is performed by the licensed professional. Final pricing, scope, and authorization are handled directly with them.

Services We Coordinate

Leak Issues

Evaluation and repair performed by licensed plumbing professionals.

Drain & Toilet Problems

Coordination of licensed service for backups, blockages, and diagnostics.

Water Heaters

Installation, repair, and replacement completed by licensed plumbers only.

Fixture Maintenance

Limited non-invasive maintenance assistance may be available where legally permitted.

Important Disclosure

Plumbing Gurus is not a plumbing contractor.

We provide coordination and dispatch services only. All plumbing work requiring licensure is performed by independently licensed plumbing professionals.

Estimates provided through this website are non-binding informational ranges. Final diagnosis, scope of work, and pricing are determined solely by the licensed plumber.
