About Plumbing Gurus

Reliable handyman and home services built on honesty, compliance, and quality workmanship in San Antonio.

Our Story

Plumbing Gurus was founded to give homeowners and property managers a dependable option for everyday home repairs and fixture replacement. Our focus has always been simple: show up on time, communicate clearly, and do the job right.

We specialize in non-plumbing fixture replacement, handyman repairs, local delivery, and property support. When licensed plumbing work is required, we coordinate with qualified independent professionals.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide reliable, transparent, and professional home services while operating in full compliance with Texas regulations. We believe trust is built through consistency and honest work.

How We Work

Every project starts with clear communication and realistic expectations. We evaluate your needs, explain available options, and only perform work that is legally permitted under our service classification.

This protects both our customers and our business.
