Compliance & Licensing Information

Plumbing Gurus is committed to operating in full compliance with Texas laws and industry regulations. This page outlines our service scope and legal standards.

Service Classification

Plumbing Gurus operates as a handyman and home maintenance service provider. We provide non-plumbing fixture replacement, hardware installation, and general home services.

We do not perform regulated plumbing, gas, sewer, or underground utility work.

Licensing Policy

All work requiring a Texas plumbing license is referred to independently licensed professionals.

We do not modify water lines, drain lines, gas lines, or sewer connections.

Fixture Replacement Policy

Fixture replacement services are limited to customer-supplied fixtures that can be replaced without altering existing plumbing infrastructure.

Any installation requiring pipe modification, soldering, cutting, or pressure testing is referred to a licensed plumber.

Insurance Coverage

Job-specific insurance coverage is arranged when required based on project scope and customer requirements.

Coverage details are disclosed prior to service when applicable.

Permits & Regulations

Customers are responsible for identifying and disclosing any permit requirements, HOA restrictions, or municipal regulations affecting their property.

We assist in coordination with licensed professionals when permits are required.

Customer Responsibility

Customers agree to provide accurate project information and disclose existing conditions that may affect service delivery.

Failure to disclose relevant conditions may result in service delays or referral to licensed providers.

Legal Notice

Information on this website is provided for general guidance only and does not constitute legal advice.

Service offerings may be modified to comply with changes in state or local regulations.
