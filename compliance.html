Compliance & Legal Information

Service Classification

Plumbing Gurus operates primarily as a handyman and home services provider. We do not perform regulated plumbing, gas, sewer, or utility work.

Licensing Policy

Any plumbing work requiring licensure under Texas law is referred directly to independently licensed professionals. Plumbing Gurus does not modify water lines, drain lines, gas systems, or sewer connections.

Referral & Dispatch Services

When specialized or regulated services are required, we may assist with scheduling and referral coordination for licensed contractors. This service is limited and separate from our handyman operations.

Insurance Coverage

Job-specific insurance coverage may be arranged when required based on project scope and client needs.

Customer Responsibilities

Customers are responsible for disclosing existing conditions, permits, HOA restrictions, and property limitations prior to service. Failure to disclose relevant information may affect service availability.

Legal Disclaimer

Information on this website is provided for general guidance only and does not constitute legal advice. Service offerings, pricing, and availability may change based on regulatory requirements.
