Compliance & Legal Information

Plumbing Gurus is a coordination and dispatch service connecting homeowners with independently licensed plumbing professionals in San Antonio and Bexar County.

Our Role

Plumbing Gurus is not a plumbing contractor. We do not perform, authorize, or employ anyone to perform regulated plumbing work. All plumbing work is completed by independently licensed plumbing professionals who are responsible for compliance with Texas regulations.

Our service consists of scheduling, coordinating, and dispatching licensed plumbers. We may provide limited, non-invasive maintenance assistance where legally permitted.

Minor Non-Regulated Work

Certain minor, non-invasive tasks may be completed by Plumbing Gurus staff. Examples include:

  • General advice or guidance
  • Surface-level maintenance that does not alter plumbing systems
Any work that would require a license under Texas law is performed only by licensed professionals.

Estimates & Pricing

All estimates provided on this website or during booking are informational only. Final pricing, scope, and authorization are determined by the licensed plumber. Coordination and scheduling fees are separate from labor or materials.

San Antonio Service Area

Our service area is limited to San Antonio and surrounding Bexar County. We coordinate only with professionals licensed in Texas.

Customer Acknowledgment

By using Plumbing Gurus services, you acknowledge and understand that:

  • We do not perform licensed plumbing work.
  • All licensed plumbing work is performed by independent licensed plumbers.
  • Coordination and scheduling fees are separate from labor or materials.
  • Estimates are non-binding informational ranges only.
