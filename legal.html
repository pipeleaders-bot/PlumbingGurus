Minor plumbing and licensed coordination

Important Information

Plumbing Gurus provides minor plumbing tasks directly, such as faucet replacements, sink unclogs, and small repairs. Any work requiring a licensed plumber is coordinated with our trusted network of professionals to ensure compliance with Texas regulations.

Limitations

Plumbing Gurus is not a licensed plumbing contractor. Major plumbing work, permits, or installations are performed by licensed professionals. Our role is to provide dispatching and coordination services for licensed plumbers while handling minor non-licensed tasks ourselves.
